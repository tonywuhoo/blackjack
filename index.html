House Wins.
Rules:
1.Dealer Shuffles 6 decks of 52 Bicycle Cards.
2. The Player is drawn 2 cards, and the house is drawn 2 with the second face down
3. The player chooses to either hit or stand. If the player card values exceed 21, the house Wins
4. Kings, Queens, Jacks are valued at 10, Aces are at 11
5. After player stands,the house goes. House stands at soft 17
6. Winner takes all.
